Q) Reverse given number ?

	Question Explanation: Reverse the given Number from 12345 to 54321;

	Solution: step1: perform modulus operation on given number with 10.
		  step2: multiply the reverse integer with 10 and add the first step result to reverse integer.
		  step3: divide the number with 10 and take floor value.
		  step4: perform this operations until given number becomes 0.
